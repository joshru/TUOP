    Features:

    • Zombies!

    • Bullets!

    • Health Pickups!

    • GODLIKE Pickups!

    • Sound Effects! (starts muted)

    • Varied zombie spawns for each wave



  • Game starts muted now, mute button located just above the canvas.
  • VOLUME WARNING!! All our sound effects are quite loud right now, you've been warned...
  • Move the player around with W A S D. Shoot with left click. Press H to toggle debug mode.

    Controls

  • W A S D to move. Shoot with left click. Hold left click to shoot as fast as the current weapon allows
  • Weapons: 1 = pistol, 2 = Assault Rifle, 3 = Shotgun, 4 = Sniper
  • Press G to toss a grenade
