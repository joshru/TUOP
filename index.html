Tales from Under the Overpass

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

    Features:

    • Zombies!

    • Bullets!

    • Health Pickups!

    • Sound Effects!

    • Fibonacci Sequence Spawning!!

  • VOLUME WARNING!! All our sound effects are quite loud right now, you've been warned...
  • Move the player around with W A S D. Shoot with left click. Press H to toggle debug mode.
  • Bugs: If a zombie is killed on top of a health drop he will pick it up for you, we tried fix it but can't find the reason right now. Will fix later.
  • Features to come: More guns, more animations, movement within a much larger map (close to finished but not polished enough for this submission), better item drops, and much more!
